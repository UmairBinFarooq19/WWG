Who We Are

Our Story

WildWays Global was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate Kashmiri travel enthusiasts who wanted to share the true magic of their homeland with the world. What started as a small taxi service between Srinagar and Gulmarg has grown into Kashmir's most-reviewed travel agency — with 287+ verified five-star reviews and thousands of happy travellers from across India and the world.

We are based in Tangmarg, Gulmarg — right at the gateway to one of the world's most spectacular mountain destinations. This means we don't just sell Kashmir tours — we live Kashmir every single day. Every guide, driver, and team member is a local who knows every road, meadow, and mountain pass intimately.

What We Stand For

Our Values

🏔️ Local Expertise

Every team member is a local Kashmiri with deep knowledge of terrain, weather, culture, and hidden gems tourists never find on their own.

🤝 Full Transparency

No hidden charges. No bait-and-switch. What we quote is exactly what you pay. All packages are fully itemised and honest.

🛡️ Safety First

All vehicles are GPS-tracked and well-maintained. Guides are trained in first aid. We monitor weather and road conditions for every trip.

💚 Sustainability

Responsible tourism — minimising waste, supporting local artisans, contributing to conservation in Gulmarg's fragile Himalayan ecosystem.
