Kashmir Honeymoon FAQs

March to June (spring/summer) is ideal for a Kashmir honeymoon — tulip gardens bloom in April, the weather is pleasant (10–25°C), and all destinations are fully accessible. December–February is magical for couples who want snow at Gulmarg. October–November offers golden chinar trees and fewer crowds. Avoid mid-July to mid-August (peak monsoon, crowded).

Yes, Kashmir is completely safe for tourists, including honeymoon couples. Major tourist areas — Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonmarg — are heavily visited by domestic and international tourists.
