Gulmarg Gondola — Asia's Highest Cable Car

The Gulmarg Gondola (Gulmarg Ropeway) is Asia's highest and longest cable car, connecting Gulmarg village to the Apharwat Peak at 3,979m. Phase 1 takes you to Kongdoori (2,650m) while Phase 2 ascends to Apharwat Peak with breathtaking Pir Panjal Range views. WildWays Global assists all guests with advance gondola ticket booking as part of every Gulmarg tour package.

Gondola tickets sell out fast in winter (Dec–Mar) and summer weekends. We recommend booking at least 2 days in advance. Our team handles all online reservations on your behalf.

About Gulmarg — The Switzerland of India

Gulmarg (meaning "Meadow of Flowers") is a hill station and ski resort located in the Baramulla district of Jammu & Kashmir, India, at an altitude of 2,650 metres. It is famous for the Gulmarg Gondola — Asia's highest and longest cable car, the Gulmarg Biosphere Reserve, and world-class skiing slopes that attract tourists from across India and internationally.

The distance from Srinagar to Gulmarg is approximately 56 km, taking about 2 hours by road via Tangmarg. WildWays Global operates daily Srinagar to Gulmarg taxi service with experienced local drivers who know the mountain roads intimately.

  • Gulmarg altitude: 2,650m (main town) to 3,979m (Apharwat Peak via gondola)
  • Best time to visit Gulmarg for snow: December, January, February, March
  • Best time for greenery and trekking: April, May, June, September, October
  • Nearest airport: Srinagar International Airport (65 km, approx. 2.5 hrs)
  • Gulmarg gondola timings: 10:00 AM – 4:30 PM (subject to weather)
