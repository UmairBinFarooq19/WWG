Gulmarg Skiing FAQs

The best skiing season in Gulmarg is December to March. January and February typically offer the deepest snow (1–3 metres in the village) and best ski conditions. December is great for early-season powder, while March offers spring skiing with warmer temperatures and longer daylight hours. Skiing is not available April–November.

No prior experience needed! Our beginner packages start with certified instructors who teach you on gentle Kongdoori slopes. Within 1–2 days, most beginners can ski independently on easy runs. For children above 6 years, special beginner lessons are available. Gulmarg's long, wide slopes make it ideal for learning.

All WildWays Global skiing packages include ski/snowboard rental, boots, poles, and helmets. High-altitude goggles and thermal ski suits can be rented additionally at ₹300–₹500/day. If you have your own equipment, we offer reduced package rates. Modern Rossignol and Fischer skis are available at the Gulmarg Ski Association rental centre.

Yes, skiing in Gulmarg is safe with proper guidance. All our instructors are certified by the Gulmarg Ski Association and trained in first aid. We always start beginners on marked, groomed slopes and only progress to off-piste with experienced guides. Helmet use is mandatory in all our packages. Avalanche-prone areas are clearly marked and off-limits without professional guides.
