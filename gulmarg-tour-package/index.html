Best Time to Visit Gulmarg – Seasonal Guide

December to March (Winter / Snowfall Season): Gulmarg transforms into a snowy wonderland. This is the best time for skiing, snowboarding, and enjoying the gondola ride through snow-covered pines. Temperatures range from -10°C to 5°C. Book early — Gulmarg hotels and gondola slots fill up fast during December–January.

April to June (Spring): Snow begins to melt, revealing emerald-green meadows carpeted with wildflowers. Ideal for trekking to Khilanmarg and Bota Pathri. Gondola operates smoothly. Comfortable 5°C to 20°C.

July to August (Peak Summer): Lush meadows, pleasant weather (10°C–25°C), and the famous Gulmarg meadow at its greenest. Perfect for families and couples. Gondola Phase 1 is always open; Phase 2 depends on weather.

September to November (Autumn): Golden chinar leaves, clear skies, and thin tourist crowds. Excellent photography conditions. Early snowfall possible in October–November at Apharwat Peak.
