Srinagar to Gulmarg Taxi FAQs

The one-way taxi fare from Srinagar to Gulmarg ranges from ₹2,500 to ₹3,500 for a private sedan (up to 4 passengers). For SUVs (Innova, Ertiga), expect ₹3,200–₹4,500. These prices include toll charges and driver charges. The journey covers 56 km and takes approximately 2 hours. WildWays Global offers fixed pricing — no negotiation needed.

Gulmarg is located 56 km from Srinagar city centre and approximately 64 km from Srinagar International Airport. The drive takes 1.5–2.5 hours depending on traffic at Tangmarg (the base town for Gulmarg). The route passes through scenic Kashmiri villages and pine forests, with mountain views opening up after Tangmarg.

There is no direct tourist bus from Srinagar to Gulmarg. The J&K State Road Transport Corporation (SRTC) runs infrequent shared bus services up to Tangmarg (47 km), from where shared jeeps/taxis cover the remaining 13 km to Gulmarg. For convenience, comfort, and reliability, a private taxi is strongly recommended. WildWays Global taxis door-to-door service from your hotel or airport.

Private vehicles are permitted up to the main parking area in Gulmarg village. Beyond that, pony/horse rides or walking is required to reach the gondola station (about 5 minutes walk). In peak winter, when roads are icy, the last 2–3 km may require snow chains. WildWays Global vehicles are always equipped with snow chains for winter visits.
