Day by Day

Sample 6N/7D Kashmir Itinerary

Click each day to see full details

Day 1 — Arrival in Srinagar

Arrive at Sheikh ul-Alam Airport. Transfer to hotel. Evening Shikara ride on Dal Lake — watching floating gardens, houseboats, and Himalayan reflections on still water. Welcome dinner with authentic Kashmiri cuisine. Overnight Srinagar.

Day 2 — Srinagar City Sightseeing

Full day Srinagar city tour: Shankaracharya Temple, Mughal Gardens (Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, Chashme Shahi), Pari Mahal, and the historic Hazratbal Shrine. Evening stroll at Lal Chowk. Overnight Srinagar.

Day 3 — Day Trip to Gulmarg

Drive to Gulmarg (56 km, ~2 hrs). Ride Asia's highest cable car — the Gulmarg Gondola — to Apharwat Peak (4,390m) for breathtaking Himalayan views. Winter option: skiing on world-class Himalayan slopes. Return to Srinagar.

Day 4 — Drive to Pahalgam

Drive to Pahalgam (96 km). En route, visit Saffron fields of Pampore. Explore Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari. Overnight in Pahalgam resort.

Day 5 — Pahalgam to Sonamarg

Drive to Sonamarg — "Meadow of Gold." Visit the Thajiwas Glacier by pony or on foot. Spectacular glaciated peaks surround you. Return to Srinagar by evening.

Day 6 — Free Day / Shopping

Leisure morning. Optional: Wazwan cooking class, carpet weaving demo, or a second shikara ride. Shop for Kashmiri shawls, dry fruits, and saffron at Lal Chowk. Farewell Wazwan dinner.

Day 7 — Departure

After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport. Take home memories of Kashmir's eternal beauty — truly Jannat on Earth.
